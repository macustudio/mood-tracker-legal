Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

Mood Tracker ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy describes what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

Account information. When you sign up, we store your email, name, date of birth, and sex (optional) for personalization and research insights.

Mood entries. Your mood ratings, feelings, activities, notes, and timestamps are stored to provide the core tracking and analytics features.

Onboarding responses. Your answers in the onboarding quiz (goals, stress symptoms, sources) are used to tailor insights and are stored with your account.

Health data (optional). If you connect Apple Health or Google Fit, we may sync steps, sleep, exercise, heart rate, and similar metrics to offer health-related insights. This data is only collected with your explicit permission.

2. How We Store Your Data

Your data is stored securely with Supabase (hosted infrastructure) and is only accessible to you and to our systems for providing the service.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following services to operate the app:

  • Supabase — Authentication and database (your account and mood data)
  • Amplitude — Usage analytics (events, screen views) to improve the product
  • Sentry — Crash reporting to fix bugs
  • Expo — Push notifications and app infrastructure
  • Apple / Google — Sign-in and, where enabled, health data

Each provider has their own privacy policy. We only share data necessary for the service they provide.

4. Push Notifications

If you enable reminders, we store your notification preferences locally and use Expo's push service to send them. We do not use push for marketing.

5. Your Rights

You can:

  • Access your data through the app
  • Delete your account in Settings → Delete Account (removes all your data)
  • Delete all entries in Settings without deleting your account
  • Opt out of analytics by disabling Amplitude via app configuration

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained until you delete your account or your entries. After account deletion, we remove your data from our systems within a reasonable period.

7. Children

The app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the latest version. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.

9. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact us at hello@macu.studio
